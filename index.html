BloomMail is a Temporary / Disposable Email application that allows users to generate temporary email addresses and receive emails. We respect user privacy and are committed to protecting it. This document outlines exactly how our app operates regarding your data.

1. Information Collection and Use

BloomMail respects your anonymity. We do not directly collect, store, or require any personal user information to use the app.

The app does NOT collect:
  • Name, Personal email address, or Phone number
  • Contacts list
  • Photos, media, or files from the device
  • Precise location or address
  • Login credentials or account details

There is no sign-up or sign-in system in the app.

2. Temporary Email Data

To provide the core functionality of disposable emails:

  • All email addresses generated in BloomMail are temporary.
  • These email addresses are not linked to real-world identities.
  • Emails and mailboxes may be automatically deleted after a certain period.

3. Internet & Permissions

BloomMail uses an internet connection only for essential features:

  • Generating temporary email addresses
  • Loading and displaying received emails
  • Displaying advertisements & processing purchases

Permissions We Do NOT Request

We believe in minimal permissions. We do NOT ask for:

  • Camera or Microphone
  • Location (GPS)
  • SMS or Call Logs
  • Storage Access (Personal files)

4. Advertisements

BloomMail is supported by advertisements (e.g., Google AdMob). These third-party networks may collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Device Type
  • General Usage Data
The developer does not have direct access to or control over data collected by advertising networks.

5. In-App Purchases

We offer optional purchases to remove ads or unlock features. All payments are processed securely by Google Play Store.

We do NOT store: Credit cards, bank details, or billing info.

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services like Google AdMob and Google Play Billing. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

BloomMail does not contain harmful content and is intended for users aged 13+. As we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of personal data leakage for minors.
